### Cross-link copy

**On every individual listing** (above the keyword footer):
> Part of the **{{Series}}** series. Save $28 with the curated set of three — see "{{Set Name}}" in our shop. Designed to balance as a gallery wall or stand alone.

**On every set listing** (top of description):
> A curated triptych from our **{{Series}}** series — three prints designed to balance as a single gallery wall. Available individually here: *{{Print 1}}*, *{{Print 2}}*, *{{Print 3}}* (linked).

### Also enable Etsy's "Mix & Match Offers" — 20% off

Auto-discounts when buyer adds all 3 individuals to cart. Doesn't replace the dedicated set listings — it catches buyers who land on an individual first. ([Etsy Help: sales & discounts](https://help.etsy.com/hc/en-us/articles/115014260108))

## 4. Branding — banner, icon, announcement, about

### Banner (3360 × 840)

Best-in-class is **single styled lifestyle hero + minimal text** ([LitCommerce 2026](https://litcommerce.com/blog/how-to-make-an-etsy-banner/)).

**Concept:** soft natural-light photograph of a pale-oak console table against a warm-white wall. Above it, a curated triptych: one sumi-e piece, one minimalist landscape, one botanical — framed in light oak. Single ceramic vase + dried branch on the console. Negative space top-right: shop name "Calm & Oak" in thin serif, tagline below: "Quiet art for considered homes." Palette: bone white, oak, charcoal ink, single warm earth tone.

Safe area is the centre ~1200 px wide — mobile crops the edges.

### Shop icon (500 × 500)

A small **enso brand mark** — single circular ink brushstroke (the unbroken circle of intentional design), charcoal on bone-white. Wordless mark — recognizable at thumbnail size next to product images.

## 5. Launch must-do / must-avoid

### MUST DO

1. **List 20+ at open, not 5.** Shops with 3–5 listings "scream newbie" and miss the algorithm's new-shop boost. Target all 30 individuals + 4+ set listings live on day one. ([growingyourcraft first 100 sales](https://www.growingyourcraft.com/blog/how-to-get-your-first-100-sales-for-your-etsy-digital-product-shop), [Marmalead 2026](https://blog.marmalead.com/etsy-print-on-demand/))

2. **Disclose Gelato as Production Partner.** Non-optional under Etsy's POD rules — failure = suspension risk. Shop Manager → About → Production Partners → add Gelato BEFORE publishing any listing. ([Marmalead 2026](https://blog.marmalead.com/etsy-print-on-demand/))

3. **Pinterest from week 1 — 10 pins per print.** Proven traffic feeder for Japandi/wall-art. Pin all 30 prints in 10 graphic variations each in the first 30 days. Inbound social links also boost Etsy organic ranking. ([Printify 2026](https://printify.com/blog/how-to-use-pinterest-for-etsy/), [growingyourcraft Pinterest 2026](https://www.growingyourcraft.com/blog/pinterest-marketing-strategies-for-etsy-sellers))

### MUST AVOID

1. **Processing time set too short.** Gelato POD takes 2–7 business days. Set "1-day processing" and you miss windows → late-delivery 1-star reviews → 1-year Star Seller exclusion. **Set 3–5 business days.**

2. **Generic / over-competed titles.** "Japandi wall art" alone has millions of listings. Use long-tail (now done — see `_gelato-listings-v2.json` v2 titles). Marmalead-validate before publish.

3. **No physical samples.** Gelato's paper, color profile, and trim vary subtly. Un-sampled launches produce "looked different IRL" reviews that tank listings forever. **Order one of each size on day 0**, photograph them, use real prints in mockups where possible.

## 6. Execution sequence (recommended order)

### Pre-launch (do BEFORE publishing any Etsy listing)

1. ✅ Title + description rewrites (done — `_gelato-listings-v2.json`)
2. ✅ Print masters publicly hosted (done — calmandoak.com/assets/img/print-files/)
3. 🚧 Order Gelato samples (8×10 of one sumi-e + one tonal + one calligraphy)
4. 🚧 Add Gelato as Etsy Production Partner
5. 🚧 Build 4 custom infographics (slots 6, 8, 9, 10) — done by Claude, uploaded by user
6. 🚧 Build banner + icon — done by Claude
7. 🚧 Write announcement + about page — done in this playbook
8. 🚧 Create 10 shop sections in Shop Manager

### Soft launch (10–14 listings, low-volume)

9. 🚧 Push the 14 missing Gelato templates to Etsy (bulk-create CSV ready)
10. 🚧 Verify all 30 individuals are Etsy drafts with full 10-image galleries
11. 🚧 Build the 10 set listings in Gelato → Etsy

### Public launch (all 40 listings live)

12. Publish drafts in batches of 5/day for 8 days (Etsy-safe pacing)
13. Enable Mix & Match Offers (20% off per series triptych)
14. Start Pinterest fresh-pin cadence (3 pins/day)
15. Watch Star Seller metrics weekly

### Weeks 3–8 — optimization

16. Review which sets vs individuals convert; rebalance promotion
17. Best Sellers section becomes data-driven (week 4)
18. Add Etsy Ads on top-3 best-sellers ($1/day each to start)
