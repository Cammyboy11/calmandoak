## The goal (winnable definition of "world leader in Japandi")
Not the biggest retailer (can't out-scale Wayfair/Article in 6 months). The **leading Japandi authority + community**:
1. **#1 editorial authority** for Japandi informational queries (beachhead: the office cluster, already pos ~12).
2. **The default cited source in AI answers** (GEO — open land-grab right now; being early compounds).
3. **The largest engaged Japandi community** (email list + Pinterest) — owned, algorithm-proof.

Own the *conversation* about Japandi, not the warehouse.

## The growth model (read before "10% a day")
Literal 10%/day is a fantasy (1.1^180 ≈ 10M×). The real engine: **compound the right ASSETS at ~5–10% per week** (≈3–11× in 6 months), and because they reinforce each other, the combined output looks like leadership. "10% better every day" = a **mindset operationalized as a daily loop**: ship → measure → learn → never repeat a loser, always double a winner.

## What compounds (pour effort here) vs. what's linear (minimize)
**Compounds:**
1. **Content library** — each article ranks/earns forever; every new one strengthens the internal-link mesh and lifts the whole site (superlinear). → 2 articles/week, office cluster first.
2. **Authority / backlinks** — one link lifts every page. → ship a **link-magnet tool every ~2 weeks** (palette generator ✅ → calculator → style quiz → room-cost estimator) + HARO drafts.
3. **AI-citation (GEO)** — compounds as AI search grows; win it while empty. → comparison / "best X for Y" guides built for extraction (FAQ + tables + schema).
4. **Email list** — owned, repeat distribution. → the tools ARE the lead magnets; grow it every week.
5. **Data / learning layer** — the unfair advantage; the fleet gets smarter weekly. → tighten the digest→KPI→reprioritize loop.

**Linear (minimize):** one-off social posts, paid ads, manual busywork. Don't let these crowd out the five above.

## The flywheel
publish → rank + get cited → traffic → email + Pinterest follows → distribution → more eyes on the next piece → backlinks → higher authority → all content ranks better → more traffic. Each turn faster + smarter than the last.

## Weekly prioritization rule (director applies this)
1. Look at what MOVED (GSC clicks/positions, Pinterest saves/clicks, email signups, affiliate revenue).
2. **Double the winner** — make more of whatever format/topic/hook is working.
3. **Kill the loser** — stop whatever's flat 3 weeks running (e.g. step social 5→3/day per the algorithm rule).
4. Spend the freed effort on the highest-compounding asset that's behind (usually: more office-cluster content, the next link-magnet tool, or a GEO guide).
5. Protect the loop: never repeat a mistake the digest already flagged.

## The one truth that decides it
The biggest predictor of leading Japandi in 6 months is **not quitting in month 2–3** — exactly when Pinterest is working and Google is about to turn on but revenue is still thin. The machine removes the human reasons people quit. Keep it pointed, keep it running.

## 6-month milestones (track against GROWTH-PLAN §milestones)
Office cluster top-10 for 2–3 terms · 90+ indexed pages · 100–300 monthly clicks · cited in AI answers for ≥2 core questions · 100–250 email subs · 3–5 quality backlinks · 100+ fresh pins/mo. Hit these and the "authority leader" position is real.
