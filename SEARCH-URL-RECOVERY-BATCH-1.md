## How to use this (≈30 sec per pin, vs the ~5 min of finding one from scratch)
For each row below I've already done the hard part — found a real product that matches the pin. You do the 10-second safety check your playbook requires:
1. Open the product link.
2. Confirm it still meets the bar: **4.4★+, 200+ reviews, Prime, japandi look.** (If it slipped, the runner-up column is your backup.)
3. Set the right **variant** (colour/size) where noted.
4. **SiteStripe → grab your affiliate link**, or just append `?tag=calmandoak-20` to the `/dp/` URL (pre-filled below).
5. Paste into the pin's destination, replacing the old `/s?k=` URL in `Product URLs.md`.
