## The launch path (lean, low-risk)

1. **Validate first.** Let the waitlist run while we drive a little Pinterest traffic to `/shop/prints/`. If sign-ups come, we invest the last mile. If not, we've spent almost nothing. **(me: drive traffic / monitor)**

2. **Pick a POD platform. (you — needs an account)**
   - **Recommended: Gelato or Printful.** Both print posters to order worldwide, no inventory, no minimums. Gelato has the widest global print network (cheaper int'l shipping); Printful integrates most smoothly with Shopify/Etsy.
   - Storefront options: connect the POD to **Shopify** (most brand control, ~$29/mo), **Etsy** (built-in traffic, fees per sale), or a simple **Payhip/Big Cartel** store (cheap). For a first product, Etsy or Shopify.

3. **Production files. (me)** The current images are styled mockups for the website. For printing I'll generate clean, flat, full-bleed versions of each design at 300 DPI in the standard print ratios (4:5 and 2:3 / ISO A) and hand you ready-to-upload files. Only worth doing for designs the waitlist actually wants.

4. **Pricing. (you, I'll advise)** Typical POD economics: an 18×24" poster costs ~$8–12 to print; sells for $30–45. Healthy margin, and it's *your* brand on it. I'd launch at a single intro price with the waitlist discount.

5. **Connect & go live. (you + me)** You create the store + connect POD + add payment; I'll swap the `/shop/prints/` page from waitlist mode to live product cards (real "Add to cart"/buy links), add Product schema with real prices, and wire it into the shop nav.

## Why prints are the right first owned product
Zero inventory, ~70%+ margin, on-brand (wall art is already a decor category), and they make the brand tangible — the moment Calm & Oak sells something only Calm & Oak makes, it stops being a curation site and becomes a brand.

The launch 4 read as a cohesive gallery wall. When validated we can expand the collection (sets/bundles sell well — "the gallery wall set of 3" at a bundle price is the highest-AOV move). Also a natural cross-sell with the affiliate frames already in the shop.
