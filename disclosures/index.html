Disclosures

How Calm & Oak makes money.

In plain English: we earn small commissions when readers buy through our links, and we tell you about every one. No hidden fees, no kickbacks, no editorial-for-sale.

Amazon Associates

Calm & Oak is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon. As an Amazon Associate, Calm & Oak earns from qualifying purchases.

What this means in practice: when you click a link to Amazon from this site and buy something — anything, not just the item linked — Amazon pays us a small commission. The price you pay is exactly the same. None of our editorial picks change because of commission rates.

Other affiliate partners

We occasionally link to a small handful of independent retailers and craftspeople through their own affiliate programs. The same rules apply: a commission to us, the same price for you, no impact on what we recommend.

Sponsored content

We work with a very small number of brand partners each quarter. Any sponsored post, sponsored image, or paid placement will be clearly labelled "Sponsored" at the top of the relevant section. Our regular editorial picks — including every "shop the look" capsule — are never sponsored.

Editorial independence

We don't accept payment to feature a product. We don't accept gifted product in exchange for coverage. If we like something, we say so; if we don't, we leave it out. Brands cannot buy their way into a capsule.

Pricing & availability

Prices and availability shown on Amazon and other retailers can change at any time. We do our best to keep links and price tiers accurate, but Amazon's price at checkout is the price that applies — please confirm before buying.
