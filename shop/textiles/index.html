Layer like a magazine, not a catalog.

The trick: every textile in the room must feel different in your hand. Heavy linen on the bed, lighter linen at the windows, wool on the seat, jute under the foot. Same neutral palette, four different textures. That contrast is what makes a Japandi room feel three-dimensional in photos.

Skip the cushion sets that come matched. Buy three different cushions, in three different weaves, in the same colour family.
