How it's made

Each piece is printed to order on natural-feel fabric — no inventory, no waste. Cushion covers, tea towels and table linens carry our own patterns in colourways we've tuned to the Japandi palette. Every design is proofed on a physical sample before it goes live: if our name is on it, it has to be worth living with.
