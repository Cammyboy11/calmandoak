Common questions

How do I buy a print?
Two ways: buy the instant digital download (from $12) and print it yourself, or order it printed to order on museum-matte paper and shipped (from $28). Each print links to both.

What sizes are available?
Every print comes in 8×10, 11×14 and 16×20 inches — sized to fit standard off-the-shelf frames. Larger sizes are on Etsy.

Are the prints framed?
No — they're unframed and sized to drop straight into standard frames. They ship flat or rolled.

Digital vs printed — what's the difference?
The digital download is an instant high-resolution file you print yourself at home or any lab. The printed version is made to order on heavyweight 200 gsm museum-quality matte paper and shipped to you.

Can I use the digital files commercially?
Digital downloads are for personal use — your own home or a gift. They aren't licensed for resale or commercial use.

Made the way we'd want them

Each print is designed in-house and made to order on heavyweight matte archival-style paper, in the sizes that fit standard frames (5×7, 8×10, 11×14, 16×20, 18×24). Unframed, shipped flat or rolled. No minimums, no waste — we only print what's ordered.
