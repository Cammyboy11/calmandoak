Three rules for framing a print well.

1. Match the size, skip the custom shop. Our prints are made to fit 8×10, 11×14, and 16×20 frames you can buy off the shelf. There is no reason to pay for custom framing.

2. A white mat is the cheapest upgrade there is. Size the frame up one step, add a mat, and a $20 print reads like a $200 piece. The margin of white does the work.

3. For a set, keep frames identical. Same size, same finish, even gaps, centred at eye level. Consistency is what turns three prints into one calm wall.
