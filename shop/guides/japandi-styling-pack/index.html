The Japandi Styling Pack is a premium PDF that turns "styling" from instinct into a system. Copy-them formulas for shelves, coffee tables and consoles; the three-weight texture rule; a print hanging and pairing guide; lighting rules for warmth; the ten do's and don'ts; and a day-by-day 30-day calm-home plan. Ten pages, instant download, print-ready — and most of it costs nothing to apply.

What's inside

Ten pages of copy-them formulas

  • The five styling principles — negative space, odd numbers, repeated materials, one living thing, styling from the chair.
  • Shelf & surface formulas — exact, copy-them recipes for the open shelf, the coffee table and the console.
