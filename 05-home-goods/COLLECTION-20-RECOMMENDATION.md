# The Calm & Oak Home Collection — 20 Patterns

*A research-backed textile & wallpaper collection. Global-craft forms and heritage motifs, rendered in the 2026 grounded-neutral palette. Built for mass appeal without breaking the calm.*

---

## The strategic insight

The loud 2026 Pinterest trends (Afrohemian, "Fun Haus," color-forward maximalism) run counter to Calm & Oak's calm. The winning move — confirmed by what's actually performing in the *textile* niche on Pinterest — is to take the **globally-trending forms and handcraft traditions** (mudcloth marks, shibori, block print, arches, checkerboard, wavy/moiré, brushwork) and render them in a **muted, timeless palette**. That's "outside the box" *and* on-brand: trend-relevant forms, heritage authenticity, mass Pinterest appeal — with Calm & Oak's quiet.

**What Pinterest textile pins are winning with right now** (observed): neutral mudcloth-style geometric marks, shoji/windowpane grids, tactile tufted-dot texture, warm color-block, organic sage abstracts.

**What the 2026 forecasts converge on:** texture over busy pattern; organic over rigid geometry; global-craft revival (shibori, mudcloth, ikat, block print, sashiko); and warm grounded neutrals.

| Clay / Terracotta | `#C97B5C` → `#B0623F` | warm accent |
| Muted ochre | `#C9A15A` | earthy accent (used sparingly) |
| Eucalyptus / Sage | `#9BA692` → `#7E8C74` | the 2026 green (Valspar "Warm Eucalyptus") |
| Indigo | `#3A4351` | deep accent (shibori / global) |
| Charcoal | `#2A2A28` | ink / contrast |

Rule holds from your brand book: **one dominant accent per pattern**, never terracotta *and* ochre together.
