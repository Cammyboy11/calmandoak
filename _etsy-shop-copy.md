### Story (5 short paragraphs, ~250 words total)

```
Calm & Oak makes prints for rooms that don't shout.

I started this studio because I wanted art that earned its wall space — pieces that hold a room rather than fill it. The aesthetic is Japandi: the Japanese discipline of negative space and ma — the considered pause — meeting the Scandinavian respect for natural materials and quiet color. None of it is loud. All of it is intentional.

The collection runs across six series. Sumi-e ink — single-breath brushstrokes in the Japanese tradition, with a vermillion hanko seal. Botanical line — one continuous ink line for a stem, an olive branch, a ginkgo leaf. Minimalist landscape — a soft horizon, a moon, a lone tree. Japanese calligraphy — Shizuka, Wa, Ma. Tonal study — oak and walnut, sage and stone. And wabi-sabi still life — three stones, a tea ceremony, light through a linen curtain.

Every print is hand-illustrated and produced on 250 gsm museum-quality matte paper — acid-free, FSC-certified, archival inks. Printed in the US and shipped flat. The paper is warm off-white, not bright white — chosen because Japandi rooms don't tolerate harsh contrasts.

Each piece can stand alone or pair into a gallery wall set. The set listings ship the three prints designed to balance together, at a saving over buying individually.

If you have a question about a piece, the paper, or a custom size — message me. I read everything.

— Calm & Oak
```

## FAQ section (optional, for shop policies page)

**Q: Is the print framed?**
A: No — all listings are unframed. Each print fits a standard frame size (8×10, 11×14, or 16×20). Frame separately for the cleanest look.

**Q: What's the paper like?**
A: 250 gsm museum-quality matte fine-art paper. Acid-free, FSC-certified, warm off-white (not bright white). The 250 gsm weight is what you'd find in a gallery print — heavy enough to feel substantial, matte enough to read without glare.

**Q: How is it printed?**
A: Printed to order on archival inks in the US through our production partner Gelato. Each print is made when you order it — nothing sits in inventory.

**Q: How long until it arrives?**
A: 3–5 business days processing + 2–5 business days US shipping = roughly one week from order to delivery.

**Q: Can I get a custom size?**
A: Yes — message me before ordering. Our base sizes are 8×10, 11×14, 16×20. Larger custom sizes are possible at 18×24, 24×36, and 30×40.

**Q: Are the gallery wall sets shipped together?**
A: Yes — the three prints in a set are produced separately but shipped together in one flat mailer.

**Q: Do you offer gift wrapping?**
A: Each print ships in a clean kraft mailer with a small thank-you card. Add a note at checkout if it's a gift and we'll skip the receipt.
