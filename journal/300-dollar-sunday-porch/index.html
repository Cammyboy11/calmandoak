The $300 Sunday porch

The porch or balcony is the most underused room in most homes. Five pieces, $300 total, and Sunday morning shifts from kitchen counter to outside.

The five pieces

1. The patio table — ~$140

Solid teak or acacia wood side table. Just big enough for two stoneware mugs, a small carafe, an open book.

The VICTORY RELAX Solid Acacia Wood Side Table (4.5★/459 reviews) on Outdoor & Wellness.

2. One outdoor chair (or two) — ~$80 each

Woven rattan or teak — the chair you can leave out year-round. Browse Outdoor & Wellness for picks.

3. The cordless outdoor lantern — ~$45

Battery LED lantern with warm bulb, 8-hour runtime. Sits on the table at dusk. The Set of 2 Cordless Table Lamps (4.5★/259 reviews).

4. The outdoor throw — ~$30

A weatherproof wool-look outdoor throw, draped over the chair. For the cooler evenings.

5. The planter — ~$30

Tall ceramic outdoor planter with a small olive tree or rosemary plant.

The math

Table $140 + chair $80 + lantern $45 + throw $30 + planter $30 = $325 (about $300 with the chair pair).
