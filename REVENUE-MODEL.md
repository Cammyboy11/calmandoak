# REVENUE-MODEL.md — the path to $2M/yr

The operating model. The marketing-director + revenue agents run the business toward this,
not just toward traffic. Read alongside NORTH-STAR.md.

## The $2M P&L shape (annual run-rate at 6–12 months)
| Stream | Target | Gating input | Owner agent |
|---|---|---|---|
| Display ads | ~$400K | 600K–1M pageviews/mo + Raptive/Mediavine | monetization |
| Affiliate (Amazon + Awin) | ~$400K | traffic + higher-commission mix | monetization + content-factory |
| Owned digital (guides, course, templates) | ~$350K | a product line + email funnel | merchandiser |
| Prints / POD | ~$250K | SKU expansion (AI image pipeline + Gelato) | merchandiser |
| DTC curated line (ceramics/linen/lighting) | ~$450K | sourcing + 3PL + checkout | merchandiser (+ Cameron ops) |
| Brand / sponsor / email promos | ~$150K | the authority + the list | director + email |

## The gating input is TRAFFIC (~600K–1M pageviews/mo)
Everything bolts onto traffic. Over-resource the flywheel (content velocity, GEO, Pinterest) —
it funds all six streams. You're early; the 6–12mo job is turning the input on.

## Sequence
- **Now → 3mo:** max traffic; launch DIGITAL products (lowest infra, ~100% margin); grow email; wire Awin + MailerLite.
- **3 → 6mo:** cross ~100K pageviews/mo → apply to Raptive; scale prints; stand up analytics + the revenue agents.
- **6 → 12mo:** launch the DTC physical line + course/membership; open brand deals — where the traffic converts to volume revenue.

## Owned now vs. unlocked by Cameron
- **Agents progress now:** content, GEO, link-magnet tools, digital products + landing pages, CRO, affiliate-mix, the analytics scaffold, the attribution standard.
- **Cameron unlocks:** MailerLite key (email sales), Awin program joins (higher commission), GSC/GA4 credential (live measurement), ad-network application (display $), DTC sourcing + 3PL + checkout (the physical line).

## The rule
Every stream is measured (see ATTRIBUTION.md). The director reviews **revenue-by-stream** weekly
and pours into the highest-margin stream that's behind, while protecting the traffic engine.
