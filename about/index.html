About

A small home journal, slowly built.

Calm & Oak began on Pinterest — quiet rooms, warm wood, the kind of homes that feel breathed-in rather than decorated. The site is the same idea, but with the products tagged so you can actually have them.

What we do

We curate Japandi-aligned home pieces — bedding, lighting, oak furniture, ceramic, paper, linen — from Amazon and a handful of trusted independent sellers, and write the room-by-room guides we wish existed when we were trying to make a Japandi home on a real budget.

What Japandi means here

Less a trend, more a rule of thumb: rooms that feel quiet. Wood you'd want to touch. Bedding that improves with age. Lighting that reads warm. One restrained colour, never two. Everything else, removed.

How we choose

Three filters. Would we buy this for our own home? Could a renter use it? Will it still look right in five years? If a piece passes all three, it makes the cut.

How we make money

Calm & Oak is reader-supported. When you buy something through one of our links, Amazon (or the relevant retailer) pays us a small commission at no cost to you. That's how the journal stays online and ad-light. Full details on the disclosures page.
