## Quality benchmarks (for Days 2-30 onward)

Use these criteria when validating products via SiteStripe yourself OR when I run the next batch.

### Metric criteria (mandatory)
- ★ 4.4+ stars
- 200+ reviews (preferred 500+)
- "X+ bought in past month" velocity visible (50+ minimum, 200+ preferred)
- Prime eligible
- Ships from Amazon (not third-party warehouse)
- "Amazon's Choice" badge is a green flag

### Visual criteria for Pinterest fit (mandatory — equally weighted)
- Amazon product photos are **clean, styled, professionally lit** (not catalog-flat or amateur)
- Material is natural: **seagrass, oak, birch, walnut, linen, wool, jute, ceramic/stoneware, rattan, brass, leather**
- Color is **warm neutral**: cream, oat, sand, charcoal, warm brown, terracotta accent
- Product is **photogenic in a styled scene** — would it look right on a light wood floor with soft window light?
- Earns its space in a pin without heavy filtering needed

### Hard rejections (regardless of star rating)
- Glossy plastic finish
- Saturated/bright colors (neon, hot pink, electric blue)
- Visible logos prominently on product surface
- Cheap-looking MDF or particleboard finish
- Cluttered Amazon product photos with multiple props/colors
- "Modern industrial" aesthetic with chrome, black metal, brushed steel
- Anything that looks like Wayfair-aesthetic-suburban
