# Calm & Oak — 150 Products for Days 1-30

**How to use this:**
1. Copy each "Amazon search query" below
2. Paste into Amazon.com search
3. Pick the top-rated result with 4.4+ stars and 500+ reviews in the right price range
4. Use **SiteStripe** (your Amazon Associates browser extension) to grab the affiliate link
5. Save the affiliate URL with the pin in your Blotato schedule

**Quality filter:** Always pick products with:
- 4.4+ star rating
- 500+ reviews (proven volume)
- Prime eligible (faster fulfillment = better experience for buyer)
- Photos that match the japandi aesthetic (cream/wood/woven, not bright colors or plastic-looking)

If your top result doesn't fit the brand aesthetic, scroll for one that does. Better to skip a product than promote one that's off-brand.

## How to use SiteStripe (the affiliate link tool)

1. Make sure you're logged into Amazon Associates ([affiliate-program.amazon.com](https://affiliate-program.amazon.com))
2. Visit any Amazon product page
3. The SiteStripe bar appears at the top
4. Click **Text** to copy the affiliate URL
5. Use that URL as the destination in your Pinterest pin

**Always test one link** by clicking it in an incognito window — it should redirect through your tracking ID. If not, your Associates account may not be approved yet.

## When to switch from Amazon to ShareASale partners (Day 60+)

For **furniture over $200** (Days 9, 24, 27, 53, 78, 93, 113, 119, 120, 135), switch to:
- **Wayfair** (via ShareASale) — 7% commission vs Amazon's 3%
- **Article** (direct) — 6-10%, modern furniture aesthetic matches japandi
- **Lovesac** — for any sofa pin, switch here. 60-day cookie + 7-10% on $1,000+ items = serious revenue

For **wall art and prints** (Day 109, 124, 144), switch to:
- **Society6** — 10%+ commission, japandi-aligned art easy to find

For **rugs** (Days 29, 112, 117, 127), switch to:
- **Rug Source** — 10% with 180-day cookie

A single Lovesac sale = $70-200 commission. That's 35-100× a typical Amazon ceramic vase commission. **At Day 60, audit your top-traffic pins and re-route the highest-AOV products to ShareASale.**
