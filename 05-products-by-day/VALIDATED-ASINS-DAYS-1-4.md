## How to use this doc

For each pin you're publishing:
1. Find the pin in the relevant launch doc (Days 1-2 in `MASTER-LAUNCH-DOC.md`, Days 3-7 in same doc, Days 8-30 in their respective docs)
2. Get the ASIN from this validated list (Days 1-4) OR use the search URL from launch doc (Days 5-7+)
3. Open `https://www.amazon.com/dp/[ASIN]` in your browser (already logged into Associates)
4. Click SiteStripe → Text → copy your affiliate URL
5. Test the link in incognito mode — should redirect through your tracking ID
6. Use that affiliate URL as the Pinterest pin destination
