## Brand color palette (use everywhere)

| Color | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Off-white background | `#F7F4EE` | Primary background |
| Warm cream alt | `#EFE8DA` | Secondary background |
| Charcoal type | `#2A2A28` | Headlines, body type |
| Soft graphite | `#5C5A55` | Sub-headlines |
| Terracotta accent | `#C97B5C` | Single accent — sparingly |
| Sand alternate | `#D4B996` | If terracotta feels too warm, swap |

**Use ONE accent only — never both terracotta and sand together.** Pick one and commit.

## Brand typeface

**Primary:** Cormorant Garamond (free, Google Fonts) — headlines + brand name
**Body:** Inter (free, Google Fonts) — small text, descriptions

If you want a single typeface for everything: **Playfair Display** (also free).

## Asset 3: Board cover images (10 boards)

Pinterest board covers display at 222×150px (and larger if user clicks in). Each cover should:
- Be visually consistent with the others (same color palette, same compositional logic)
- Use a single hero image (lifestyle photo) with no overlay text
- Feel like a curated mood, not a stock photo

### Board 1: Calm Living Rooms
**Brief:** Overhead 3/4 angle of a japandi living room corner. Light oak coffee table, cream linen sofa edge visible, single ribbed ceramic vase with eucalyptus stem. Soft morning window light from the left. Off-white wall background. Minimal — three objects max. No people.

### Board 2: Quiet Bedrooms
**Brief:** Low-angle bedroom shot. Linen-covered platform bed (sheets pre-washed, slightly rumpled), single warm-light bedside lamp on a wood nightstand, paper-shade lamp glow. Cream walls. Early-morning soft light. No clutter.

### Board 3: Japandi Kitchens
**Brief:** Tight crop of a kitchen counter corner. Wooden utensils in a stoneware crock, a small ceramic canister, a single olive branch in a clear glass vase. Light oak counter or marble. Soft light. No text.

### Board 4: Storage That Disappears
**Brief:** Closet or shelving close-up. Three woven seagrass baskets stacked or aligned, oak shelf with neatly folded linen towels visible. Cream background. Clean, ordered, but warm.

### Board 5: Soft Lighting
**Brief:** Evening shot. A paper-shade pendant lamp glowing warm, casting soft shadows on a linen-covered surface below. Charcoal-darker background to make the lamp glow stand out. Mood-piece feel.

### Board 6: Hand-Thrown Ceramics
**Brief:** Three ceramic vessels in a row — one tall ribbed vase, one short bowl, one small bud vase. Different textures (matte, ribbed, smooth). Cream background. Side lighting to emphasize texture. No flowers — just the vessels.

### Board 7: Linen & Wool
**Brief:** Stacked folded textiles. Cream linen on top, cream wool throw under, jute rug peeking at the bottom edge. Tactile, layered. Soft natural light. Almost editorial-magazine feel.

### Board 8: Calm Reading Corners
**Brief:** Reading nook angle. Linen reading chair partially in frame, oak side table with a single book and a paper-shade reading lamp glowing. A potted snake plant or olive tree edge. Warm afternoon light.

### Board 9: Small Space Calm
**Brief:** Studio-apartment corner. Compact wood console with linen storage cubes underneath, a small framed art print, a single ceramic bud vase. Off-white walls. Felt small but not cramped — intentional.

### Board 10: Tea & Wellness Rituals
**Brief:** Tea ritual setup. Cast iron teapot, two stoneware tea cups, a bamboo tray, a single fresh tea leaf or eucalyptus stem. Top-down angle. Cream linen surface. Minimal.

**All 10 board covers share:**
- Off-white / cream / wood neutral palette
- Soft morning or afternoon light (never harsh midday)
- 3 objects maximum per shot — never overstuffed
- No text overlay
- Photographic look (NOT illustrated, NOT obviously AI-generated)

**Negative prompts to use:** no warped geometry, no garbled text, no extra fingers, no oversaturation, no AI-glossy plastic look, no people, no faces, no obvious AI artifacts.

**Sizes:** Generate at 1500×1500 (square — Pinterest auto-crops to fit board cover).

## Asset 4: Pin design templates (5 layouts)

These are reusable templates you'll fill with content for each pin. Make them in Canva (free) so you can swap text/products easily.

### Template A — Single-product hero (USE FOR ~50% OF PINS)
- Canvas: 1000×1500
- Background: Off-white `#F7F4EE` solid OR a subtle linen texture
- **Top 30%:** Hook text — Cormorant Garamond bold, 80pt, charcoal `#2A2A28`, centered
- **Middle 50%:** Single product photo, cropped close, on neutral background. Real Amazon product image works fine here.
- **Bottom 20%:** Sub-hook in Inter Regular 24pt graphite `#5C5A55`. Brand wordmark in lower right (12pt, 60% opacity)

### Template B — Lifestyle scene
- Canvas: 1000×1500
- Full-bleed lifestyle photo (japandi room shot)
- Hook overlay in upper third on a darker zone of the image
- Brand wordmark bottom right
- (For these, you'll need actual room scene photos — generate via Nano Banana 2 once billing is on, OR pull from royalty-free sites like Unsplash for placeholders)

### Template C — Round-up grid (4-product)
- Canvas: 1000×1500
- Top 15%: Hook — "[Number] [products] under $[price]"
- Middle 70%: 2×2 grid of 4 product images, each in its own off-white box with thin charcoal border, $ price visible below each
- Bottom 15%: Sub-hook + brand wordmark

### Template D — Before/after split
- Canvas: 1000×1500
- Top half: "BEFORE" labeled photo (a messy/empty room)
- Bottom half: "AFTER" labeled photo (the same room styled with japandi pieces)
- Hook diagonal across the midpoint divider line
- Brand wordmark bottom right
- (Hard to do without actual before/after photos — save this template for later when you have real customer photos)

### Template E — Detail + context
- Canvas: 1000×1500
- Top 60%: Tight detail shot (texture of a fabric, grain of wood, glaze of ceramic)
- Bottom 40%: Wider context shot (the same product in its room context)
- Hook on the boundary line between zones
- Brand wordmark bottom right

**Save these as Canva templates.** Once made, swapping content per pin takes 90 seconds vs 30 minutes from scratch.
