Privacy

Privacy policy.

Last updated: May 2026. The short version: we collect very little, we don't sell anything, and you can email us to delete what we have.

What we collect

  • Email address — only if you sign up for the newsletter or download a free guide.
  • Anonymous analytics — page views, country, device type. We do not collect personally identifying information through analytics.
  • Cookies — only the minimum required for the site to function and for affiliate-link tracking.

How we use it

Email addresses are used to send you the guide you requested, plus an occasional newsletter (about once a fortnight). You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in any email — we'll remove your address from our list.

Analytics data helps us understand which articles are useful so we can write more of them.

Third parties we use

  • Email provider — to send the newsletter and free guides (subject to their own privacy policy).
  • Analytics provider — privacy-respecting analytics.
  • Affiliate networks — Amazon Associates and a small number of independent affiliate programs use cookies to credit purchases.
  • Hosting — Cloudflare, which sees standard server logs (IP address, request URL, user agent).

Your rights

You can ask for a copy of any data we hold about you, or ask us to delete it. Email hello@calmandoak.com and we'll handle it within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything material, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top of this page and (if relevant) email subscribers.
