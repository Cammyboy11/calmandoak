---

## THE TWO PHASES

### Phase 0 — Seeding (Days S1–S7) — 7 days BEFORE first affiliate pin
**Purpose:** Calibrate Pinterest's algorithm. Establish your aesthetic. Fill all 10 boards with 3-4 pins each so no board is empty when affiliate pins start. **No affiliate links. No "tap to shop." Pure mood content.**

Why this matters: Pinterest's algorithm scores new accounts on consistency BEFORE it pushes them to followers. A new account with empty boards + sudden monetization = throttled. A new account with 35 beautiful seed pins distributed across all 10 boards = "this creator is established, push their content."

### Phase 1 — Affiliate (Days 1–30) — 150 product pins, 5/day
After Phase 0 finishes, the affiliate engine starts. Every pin has a product link (validated via SiteStripe).
